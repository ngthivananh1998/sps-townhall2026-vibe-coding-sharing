Vibe Coding la gi?

Dinh nghia

Vibe Coding

Ban mo ta dieu ban muon bang ngon ngu tu nhien,
AI viet code cho ban.

Khong can

  • Hoc ngon ngu lap trinh
  • Hieu cau truc du lieu
  • Biet framework hay library

Chi can

  • Biet cach dien dat

Loi ich cho MoMo

2 nhom loi ich khi nhan vien tu build

Nhom 1 — Prototype nhanh

App can du lieu tu HRM, can SSO, can he thong noi bo MoMo

  • Tu idea → clickable prototype: 1-2 ngay thay vi cho sprint
  • Test voi user that truoc khi dau tu dev time
  • PRD co demo that — stakeholder approve nhanh hon

Non-tech lam UI + mock data. Dev lo backend sau.

Nhom 2 — Build full app luon

App khong dung den he thong core cua MoMo

  • Data cua rieng team (khong lay tu HRM/core)
  • Auth tu quan ly (Google login, Supabase)
  • Deploy ra ngoai (Vercel) — khong can MoMo infra

Day moi la leverage that su cho non-tech.

3 Level loi ich

Tu ca nhan den to chuc

Level 1 — Ca nhan

Tu giai quyet pain point

Khong phu thuoc IT, khong cho sprint. Workload giam, ownership tang.

Level 2 — Team

Moi BU tu build tool

Workflow rieng, tool rieng — khong can share dev resource voi toan cong ty.

Level 3 — To chuc

Tang toc khong tang headcount

MoMo giai quyet van de nhanh hon. Non-tech tro thanh nguoi build, khong chi nguoi request.

"Voi vibe coding — neu app khong dung den he thong core cua MoMo, nhan vien khong biet code van co the tu build va ship mot san pham hoan chinh. Khong phai prototype. Khong phai cho IT. Mot minh, trong vai ngay."

Giai thich don gian

Frontend & Backend hoat dong voi nhau nhu the nao?

Frontend — Phan khach hang nhin thay

Giao dien, nut bam, form nhap lieu, bang bieu. Giong nhu mat tien cua quan an — cho do dep, menu, ban ghe.

Backend — Phan chay phia sau

Luu du lieu, xu ly logic, gui email, phan quyen. Giong nhu bep cua quan an — noi nau mon an, luu cong thuc, tinh hoa don.

Khi ban click "Luu" tren app:

Ban nhin thay form (Frontend)
→
Gui du lieu ve may chu (Backend)
→
Luu vao database (Supabase)
Buoc quan trong nhat

Product Spec — "Ban thiet ke" truoc khi xay nha

Product Spec la gi?

Mot file mo ta chi tiet app ban muon lam: lam gi, cho ai, co tinh nang gi, khong co gi. Giong nhu "hop dong" giua ban va AI.

Tai sao can co?

  • Khong co spec = AI code di code lai = mat gap 3-5x thoi gian
  • Co spec = "Code theo cai nay" → AI hieu ngay, lam dung ngay

Cach de co?

Nho AI dat cau hoi cho ban. Tra loi xong → AI tao spec. Ban review → chot.

Vai tro trong quy trinh

Ban co y tuong mo ho
↓
AI hoi ban 10-15 cau
↓
Ban tra loi (chua biet cung ok)
↓
AI tao Product Spec
↓
Ban review → Bat dau code!

Phan 6

Reflection

Nhung dieu AI khong lam thay duoc

Vibe coding giup minh di tu idea den san pham that nhanh hon rat nhieu. Nhung ngoi nhin lai qua trinh build — minh nhan ra thu AI khong lam thay duoc khong phai la code.

La biet minh dang giai quyet van de gi. La cau hoi dat ra truoc khi bat tay lam. La quyet dinh cai gi khong can build.

AI se ngay cang pho bien — den mot luc nao do, dung AI thanh thao se binh thuong nhu dung Excel bay gio. Ai cung lam duoc. Khoang cach do se thu hep lai.

Nhung khoang cach ve tu duy thi khong. Ai dang giai dung van de? Ai dang build dung thu nguoi dung can? Ai du tinh de khong tin AI mot cach mu quang?

Do van la con nguoi. AI chi la cong cu — cong cu tot den dau cung phu thuoc vao nguoi cam no biet minh muon di dau.
